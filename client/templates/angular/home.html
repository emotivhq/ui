What is it and how does it work?

Ever been a part of a "group gift"? Ever been the person in charge of collecting all the money for that gift? It's not fun having to ask that one friend, who never seems to have cash on him, or thinks "I'll get you back when we go out for drinks" is a valid form of payment.

And what if you're the "out of town" friend, or the "my boss always makes me work weekends" friend who always contributes to group gifts, but is rarely present

for the actual giving of the gift? It's a bummer to wonder if the recipient even knew you were in on it. GiftStarter puts the fun and convenience back into the gifting experience. With our simple funding campaign builder, it's easy for friends and family to come together and purchase items from the web. Once campaigns complete, we send the gift along with a beautiful, hand-crafted card including every one's well wishes. The GiftStarter process is just 5 simple steps:

1

Find a Product

GiftStarter© Beta is compatible with products from any of the retailers listed below!

2

Copy+Paste Link

Find something you love? Copy/paste the link into the box at the top of this page.

3

Become a Gift Champion

Whether the gift is for you or someone else, you're this gift's champion! Give us some info so we can make dreams come true.

4

Share With Friends

Share your special GiftStarter page with friends and family via Facebook, Twitter, or email to start the gifting!

5

Complete and Ship

As people pitch in, your GiftStarter page will show the progress. Once Gifting has completed, the product is shipped to the giftee with a special "Contributor Card."
