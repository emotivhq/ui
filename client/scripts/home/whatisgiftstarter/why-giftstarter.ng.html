Why GiftStarter?

GET AND GIVE GIFTS YOU WANT

We remove the barrier of large price tags. The gifts most people want are more expensive than the average giver is able to gift alone, but with GiftStarter, many people can give a little and together give gifts people actually want. Imagine a world where birthdays aren't just an assortment of gift cards and $20 checks from grandma.

HAVE MEANINGFUL INTERACTIONS

We didn't invent group gifting, but we did make sure the recipient will know exactly who was involved in the gift. Each participant can add a picture and personal message to the handcrafted group card. Once the gift is received, the recipient can post a thank you note and picture on the gift campaign page for all the contributors to see.

PAIN-FREE GROUP GIFTING

Whether it's your coworkers in your office or buddies spread across the nation, it's never been so easy to start a group gift. We don't just take care of the money collection, but also ensure the gift is ordered and shipped, along with the group card. And as always, shipping is free for all GiftStarter gifts!
