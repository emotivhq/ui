How it works

IF YOU CAN BUY IT ONLINE, YOU CAN GIFT IT WITH GIFTSTARTER. When you set up your gift campaign, you can choose how many tiles will be in your grid. We break up the price of the product into tiles, so the more tiles you have, the cheaper each tile is! Once your gift campaign is set up, you invite your friends to buy tiles -- and your part is finished! Each contributor can purchase as many or as few tiles as they wish, then leave a message for the group card when they check out. We take care of everything else -- from ensuring the gift is ordered and shipped to making and sending the homemade group card, we've got you covered!
