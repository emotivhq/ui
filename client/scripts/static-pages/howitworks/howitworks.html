Glossary

We’re a new concept, so we’ve needed new words to describe us.

GIFTSTART- v. the action of setting up a gift campaign
NUDGE - v. send a reminder to pitch in on a campaign
TAG - n. descriptors and filters applied to saved gift ideas
CONNECTION - n. a person who is connected to you
BUNDLE - n. a custom collection of products to be gifted
GIFTSTARTER - n. where good gifts come from
PITCH IN - v. purchasing one or more tiles from a campaign
GIFT CHAMPION - n. the person who set up the campaign
CONTRIBUTORS - n. people who have pitched in on a gift
RECIPIENT - n. the lucky person who gets the gift
CAMPAIGN - n. the method to fund great gifts***
