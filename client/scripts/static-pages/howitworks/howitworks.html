Group Gifting Starts Here

GiftStarter is a new gifting movement focused on bringing people together in a community that loves giving amazing gifts.

Our patent-pending technology divides the price of the gift into perfect tiles, giving family and friends the ability to purchases as many or as few tiles as they wish! Any product available online can be gifted with GiftStarter, and we’re here to help every step of the way. From collecting the money to shipping the gift - and even creating a special card with your personal messages - we’ve got you covered!

1
FIND A GIFT

Search from over 3 million products on our site. You can also discover our favorite gifts on our Gift Ideas page or contact our Gift Concierge for custom gifts.

2
SELECT A GIFT

From the search results or Gift Ideas page, you can click on product images to view product information and details, and the “GiftStart it” button. Click this button to start your group gift!

3
ADJUST THE TILES

Click the “+” to add tiles, thus lowering the price of each tile. Click the “-” button to remove tiles, and increase the price of each tile. Consider the size of the group you’ll invite; some people will buy 1 tile, some may buy more.

4
YOUR GIFTING EVENT

Describe the reason for this gift. What’s the event? Who is it for? Why is this a good gift for this person and/or event?

5
YOUR GIFTING EVENT

Tell us where this gift needs to go and when you want it there and we’ll make it happen!
*Note standard shipping is always free with GiftStarter

That’s all there is to it. Share and invite friends and family to purchase tiles and give a gift that shows how much you care. We’re here to help you at any time and to ensure the gift and personal card are delivered.
