Giftstarter Glossary

GiftStarter is a new gifting movement bringing people committed to giving amazing gifts.

Welcome to GiftStarter! Here's a glossary of gifting words to help you in our gifting community:

Gift Champion This is a person (you!) that starts an amazing gift, and invites family and friends to pitch in.
Contributor The people invited in by the Gift Champion to pitch in on the amazing gift and sign the card.
Recipient The person(s) that receive the amazing gift!
GiftStarter That's us! We are here to help you give amazing gifts you are proud to give, and they are happy to get.
Gift Concierge Our gift concierge is like your personal shopper who will help you find and give that amazing gift.
Nudge We will give you friendly reminders to help make your gifting event amazing.
